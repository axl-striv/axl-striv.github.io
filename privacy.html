Introduction

Striv™ is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including sensitive health and location data gathered through our smart insoles, mobile app, and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

Personal Identifiers

Name, email, shipping address, and payment information.

Health & Activity Data

Sensor data from Striv™ insoles including pressure, gait, stride, impact, step frequency, and related biometric insights.

Device & App Usage Data

App interactions, session duration, device type, OS, crash logs, and diagnostics.

Location Data

With your explicit consent, we collect GPS data to enhance training insights, route tracking, and performance analytics.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand usage patterns and improve your experience.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable insole tracking, AI-powered coaching, and personalized insights.
  • Improve Product & Support: Analyze aggregated usage data to enhance hardware/software performance and resolve bugs.
  • Communicate with You: Send relevant updates, service notifications, and (if opted in) marketing content.
  • Comply with Law: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We only share it with:

  • Trusted Vendors (e.g., cloud storage, payment processors, analytics providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities, if required by law.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular security audits to protect your information.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or correct your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location or health data tracking at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Object to certain types of processing (e.g., marketing)

To exercise these rights, email: info@striv.run

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as needed. The latest version will always be available at https://striv.run/privacy.html. If material changes occur, we'll notify you via email or app alert.
